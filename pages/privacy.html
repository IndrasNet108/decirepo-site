How DeciRepo processes operational and verification data.

Controller

Data We Process

Verification Requests
Decision IDs, artifact IDs, and protocol request metadata.
Operational Logs
IP address, user agent, timestamp, endpoint, and response status.
Contact Messages
Email content submitted directly to the operator.

Purpose

Run and secure verification endpoints.
Reliability
Detect abuse, failures, and protocol misuse.
Compliance

Operational logs are retained only as long as needed for security, reliability, and compliance duties, then deleted or aggregated.

Third Parties
